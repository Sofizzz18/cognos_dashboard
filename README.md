## 📁 Projects

### 1. Sales Analytics Dashboard
**Tools:** IBM Cognos Analytics & Google Looker Studio

Built two versions of a sales dashboard analyzing automotive dealer performance across 10 dealers and 5 vehicle models.

**Key Metrics:**
- Total Profit: $78.4M
- Total Units Sold: 58,118
- Average Quantity Sold: 19.37 per transaction

**Highlights:**
- KPI scorecards for at-a-glance business metrics
- Profit by Dealer ID — Dealer 1288 identified as top performer (~$9.5M)
- Quantity sold breakdown by model (Labrador, Beaufort, Champlain leading)
- Same analysis built in **both Cognos and Looker Studio** to demonstrate tool versatility

📄 [View Cognos Dashboard](./Sales2.pdf) | [View Looker Studio Dashboard](./google_looker.pdf)

### 2. Service Analytics Dashboard
**Tool:** IBM Cognos Analytics

Built an interactive service dashboard analyzing vehicle recalls and customer sentiment across 5 car models.

**Key Insights:**
- Recall analysis across 14 affected systems (airbags, brakes, engine, etc.)
- Beaufort model has highest airbag recalls (7); Salish leads on tire recalls (5)
- Sentiment analysis on 59,000+ customer responses — majority positive
- Monthly profit and sales trends tracked across full year
